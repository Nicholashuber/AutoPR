A fruit salad can be made with a variety of fruits depending on your preferences and what's in season. Here are some popular fruits that you can consider including in your fruit salad:

1. Apples
2. Bananas
3. Grapes
4. Strawberries
5. Blueberries
6. Raspberries
7. Pineapple
8. Mango
9. Kiwi
10. Oranges
11. Watermelon
12. Cantaloupe
13. Honeydew melon
14. Pears
15. Peaches

Feel free to mix and match these fruits based on your taste preferences. You can also add a squeeze of lemon or lime juice to prevent the fruits from browning and enhance the flavor. Enjoy your fruit salad!
